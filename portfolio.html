Movie Soundtrack Finder

If youre a big movie buff you may wonder where you can find information about the movies you like including the soundtrack. Instead of googling and getting a million irrelevant results, you can use this simple movie info finder. Save movies to your library for easy access to all the information you could need at your finger tips.
